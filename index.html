Triagewall

Local-LLM alert triage for self-hosted SOCs.

Pre-release. Triagewall is in active development toward v0.1. Star the repo to be notified when it's ready to install. First public release expected mid-2026.

If you run Suricata on OPNsense or pfSense, you know the problem. Hundreds of alerts a day, the vast majority of which are noise. The signal is in there, but you're not going to find it at 11 PM.

Triagewall is a local-LLM triage layer for the open-source SIEM stack: Wazuh, Suricata, and Ollama. Pre-filter the obvious noise. Send the gray area to a local model. Stop drowning in alerts.

Free and AGPL-3.0. Runs on your hardware. No telemetry.
